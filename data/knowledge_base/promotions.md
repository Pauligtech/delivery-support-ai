# DoorDash Dasher Support — Promotions & Incentives

## Overview
This article explains how DoorDash promotions, challenges, and peak pay work,
and how to resolve disputes related to missing incentive payouts.

---

## How Peak Pay Works

Peak pay is additional earnings offered during high-demand periods (lunch, dinner,
weekends, bad weather). It is displayed as a badge in the Dasher app.

**Key Facts:**
- Peak pay is added **per delivery** during the active promotion window
- It appears separately in your Earnings Breakdown
- Peak pay is **guaranteed** once you accept an order during a peak pay window

**If Peak Pay is Missing:**
1. Verify the delivery was accepted **while the peak pay badge was active** in the app
2. Check Earnings → Order History → [Order] for the peak pay line item
3. If it appears missing, submit a Pay Review request via in-app Support chat

---

## How Challenges Work

Challenges are bonus earning opportunities (e.g., "Complete 20 deliveries this week
and earn an extra $50").

**Important Rules:**
- Challenges must be **explicitly activated** by tapping "Accept Challenge" in the app
- Only deliveries completed during the challenge window count
- Challenges cannot be retroactively applied

**If Challenge Bonus is Missing:**
1. Confirm you accepted the challenge before starting deliveries
2. Verify you met the exact completion criteria (count and time window)
3. Bonuses are paid within **72 hours** of the challenge period ending
4. If the bonus is still missing after 72 hours, contact Support with your
   challenge ID (found under **Promotions → Completed Challenges**)

---

## Referral Bonuses

Dashers earn referral bonuses when they refer new Dashers who complete a minimum
number of deliveries within a set time window.

**Payout Timeline:**
- Referral bonuses are paid within **2 weeks** of the referred Dasher meeting
  the minimum delivery threshold
- Both Dasher and new Dasher must be in an active, non-suspended status at payout

**If Referral Bonus is Missing:**
1. Confirm the referred Dasher completed the required number of deliveries
   within the referral window
2. Check **Account → Referrals** for the payout status
3. If status shows "Pending" beyond 14 days, contact Support with both Dasher IDs

## Dasher Rewards Program

Active Dashers can access tiered benefits (Silver, Gold, Platinum) based on
acceptance rate, completion rate, and customer rating.

**Tier Requirements (approximate — may vary by market):**
| Tier | Acceptance | Completion | Rating |
|------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Silver | ≥ 70% | ≥ 80% | ≥ 4.2 |
| Gold | ≥ 80% | ≥ 90% | ≥ 4.6 |
| Platinum | ≥ 90% | ≥ 95% | ≥ 4.7 |

**Benefits include:** Priority scheduling, exclusive high-value orders, early access
to new markets. Visit **Account → Dasher Rewards** to check your current tier.
