# DoorDash Dasher Support — Pay & Earnings Issues

## Overview
This article covers common pay-related issues reported by Dashers, including missing
payments, earning discrepancies, and adjustment requests.

---

## Problem: Dasher did not receive payment for a completed delivery

**Symptoms:**
- Delivery marked complete in the app, but earnings not reflected
- Payment missing after 24 hours of delivery completion

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Open the Dasher app and navigate to **Earnings → History**
2. Confirm the delivery appears and is marked **"Completed"**
3. If the delivery is missing entirely, tap **Help → Report a Missing Delivery**
4. If it shows "Completed" but with $0, submit a **Pay Adjustment Request** via the
   Support Portal at [support.doordash.com](https://support.doordash.com)
5. Pay adjustments are reviewed within **3–5 business days**

**Escalation:** If the issue persists after 5 business days, contact Dasher Support
via the app chat and reference your delivery ID.

---

## Problem: Dasher's earnings are lower than expected

**Possible Causes:**
- Order cancellation after pickup (partial pay may apply per DoorDash policy)
- Customer-reported "no contact delivery" adjustments
- Incorrect mileage calculation

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Review your **Earnings Breakdown** in the Dasher app for the specific order
2. Check if any **order adjustments** were applied and their reason codes
3. Peak pay and promotions are calculated at **end of the week** (Sunday midnight)
4. If you believe a calculation error occurred, submit a Pay Review request through
   the in-app Support chat with the order ID and expected amount

**Important:** DoorDash cannot guarantee specific earning amounts per delivery.
Earnings are based on base pay + tips + any active promotions.

---

## Problem: Direct deposit not received by expected date

**Standard Processing Timeline:**
- Earnings from Monday–Sunday are processed the **following Tuesday**
- Bank processing may add an additional **1–3 business days** depending on your bank

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Verify your bank account details are correct: **Dasher App → Account → Bank Info**
2. Check for any "Payment Hold" notices in the app
3. If your payment is more than 5 business days late, file a **Missing Payment Report**
   via the Support Portal

**Note:** DoorDash does not control bank processing times once a transfer is initiated.

---

## Problem: Dasher wants to update bank account information

**Steps:**
1. Open Dasher App → **Account → Banking**
2. Tap **Edit** and enter new routing and account number
3. Changes take effect for the **next payment cycle**
4. You will receive a confirmation email to your registered address

**Security Notice:** DoorDash will never ask for your banking credentials via chat
or email. Report any such requests to security@doordash.com.
