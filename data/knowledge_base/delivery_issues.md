# DoorDash Dasher Support — Delivery & Order Issues

## Overview
This article covers problems Dashers encounter during active deliveries,
including order problems, customer disputes, restaurant issues, and safety concerns.

---

## Problem: Restaurant says the order was already picked up

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Do NOT leave the restaurant without the order or a resolution
2. Ask the restaurant manager to verify with DoorDash merchant support:
   **1-855-973-1040**
3. In the Dasher app, tap **"Can't Pick Up"** → **"Restaurant has an issue"**
4. DoorDash will contact the merchant to verify and re-assign or cancel the order
5. You will receive **half the delivery pay** if the order is cancelled due to a
   restaurant error after you arrive

---

## Problem: Customer is not responding at drop-off

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Attempt to call/text the customer using the in-app contact feature
2. Wait at the delivery location for **5 minutes** after contacting
3. If no response, follow your delivery instructions:
   - If "Hand it to me" is selected: tap **"Customer Unavailable"** in the app
   - If "Leave at door" is selected: leave the order safely and take a photo
4. Take a clear photo of the order placement using the in-app camera
5. You will receive full delivery pay regardless of customer responsiveness

---

## Problem: Dasher was involved in an accident during a delivery

**CRITICAL — SAFETY ISSUE:**
This must be handled immediately by a human Dasher Support Specialist.

**Immediate Steps for the Dasher:**
1. Ensure your safety first — move to a safe location if possible
2. Call emergency services (911) if there are injuries or vehicle damage
3. In the Dasher app, tap **"Help"** → **"I was in an accident"** to notify DoorDash
4. Do NOT admit fault or discuss insurance with the other party
5. A DoorDash Safety Specialist will contact you within **2 hours**

**Insurance:** Dashers are covered by DoorDash's commercial auto insurance while
actively on a delivery. Details: [safety.doordash.com](https://safety.doordash.com)

---

## Problem: Dasher received a low rating from a customer

**About Ratings:**
- Your Dasher rating is a rolling average of your last **100 rated deliveries**
- Ratings below **4.2** may affect your eligibility for high-value orders

**Disputing a Rating:**
1. In the app: **Ratings → tap the specific delivery → Report Inaccurate Rating**
2. Valid dispute reasons include: customer error, safety issue, restaurant delay
3. DoorDash reviews disputes within **5 business days**
4. Ratings given during restaurant delays or platform errors may be removed

**Note:** DoorDash does not remove ratings simply because they are low — only if
they violate platform policies or are factually incorrect.

---

## Problem: Dasher marked an order as delivered but customer claims non-delivery

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Provide your delivery photo (automatically saved) via **Order History → [Order] → Photo**
2. DoorDash investigates all non-delivery claims before taking action against a Dasher
3. Repeated non-delivery reports may trigger an account review
4. If you believe the customer filed a false claim, contact Dasher Support
   with the order ID and your GPS delivery confirmation

**Policy:** Dashers are protected from false customer reports. Your account will not
be penalised based on a single unverified claim.
