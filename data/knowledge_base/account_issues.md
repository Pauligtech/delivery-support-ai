# DoorDash Dasher Support — Account & App Issues

## Overview
This article covers issues with Dasher account access, app functionality,
deactivations, and profile settings.

---

## Problem: Dasher cannot log into the Dasher app

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Ensure you are using the **Dasher app** (not the customer DoorDash app)
2. Check your internet connection — switch between WiFi and mobile data
3. Try resetting your password via **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen
4. Clear the app cache: **Phone Settings → Apps → Dasher → Clear Cache**
5. Uninstall and reinstall the latest version of the Dasher app

**If none of the above work:** Contact Dasher Support via
[dasher.doordash.com/help](https://dasher.doordash.com/help)

---

## Problem: Dasher account has been deactivated

**IMPORTANT:** Account deactivation issues MUST be handled by a human Dasher Support
Specialist. The AI assistant will not attempt to resolve deactivation cases.

**What the Dasher should do:**
1. Check your registered email for a deactivation notice with the stated reason
2. You have the right to **appeal** the deactivation within **90 days**
3. Submit your appeal at: [appeals.doordash.com](https://appeals.doordash.com)
4. Appeals are reviewed within **10–15 business days**

**Escalation:** A Dasher Support Specialist will review your account history.
Do not create a new Dasher account — this may result in a permanent ban.

## Problem: Dasher's completion rate has dropped unexpectedly

**About Completion Rate:**
- Your completion rate is the percentage of accepted orders you complete
- Cancelling after pickup significantly impacts this metric

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Review recent cancellations in: **Earnings → Order History**
2. If a cancellation was marked incorrectly (e.g., restaurant closed), tap the order
   and select **"Report an Issue"**
3. Incorrect cancellation markings are reviewed within **3 business days**

**Thresholds:** Dashers with a completion rate below **80%** may face reduced order
eligibility. Rates below **70%** may trigger an account review.

## Problem: Dasher app is crashing or freezing

**Quick Fixes:**
1. Force-close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your phone
3. Ensure you are running the **latest version** of the Dasher app (check your app store)
4. Free up storage space if your device has less than **1GB available**
5. Disable any battery optimization settings that may be killing the app in background

**If the issue persists:**
- Report the bug in-app: **Menu → Help → Report a Technical Issue**
- Include your device model, OS version, and a description of what triggers the crash

---

## Problem: Dasher's GPS or navigation is inaccurate

**Resolution Steps:**
1. Enable **High Accuracy** location mode on your phone:
   - Android: **Settings → Location → Use High Accuracy**
   - iPhone: **Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Dasher → Always**
2. Calibrate your GPS: open Google Maps or Apple Maps and walk a few steps
3. Avoid tunnels, underground parking, and dense urban areas where GPS signal degrades
4. If a pickup or drop-off address is incorrect, report it:
   **Order Screen → Report Wrong Address**

**Note:** DoorDash uses third-party mapping services. Address errors are forwarded
to the mapping provider but may take several weeks to reflect.
