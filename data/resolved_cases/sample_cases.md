# Resolved Case: Missing Pay After Completed Delivery

**Case ID:** RES-2024-0042
**Category:** Pay / Earnings
**Resolution:** Pay Adjustment Approved

## Dasher's Issue
Dasher completed a delivery but the $8.50 base pay did not appear in earnings
after 48 hours. The delivery showed as "Completed" in order history.

## Investigation
Support confirmed the delivery was completed and GPS data verified drop-off.
A billing system sync error caused the payment to be queued but not processed.

## Resolution
Pay adjustment of $8.50 was issued. Dasher received payment within 2 business
days of the support ticket being filed.

## Lesson for KB
Sync errors can delay individual payments by 24–72 hours beyond the normal
processing window. Dashers should wait 72 hours before filing a support ticket
for individual missing payments, unless the amount exceeds $20.

# Resolved Case: Peak Pay Not Applied

**Case ID:** RES-2024-0117
**Category:** Pay / Promotions
**Resolution:** Partial adjustment — Dasher error confirmed

## Dasher's Issue
Dasher claimed peak pay of $3 per delivery was not applied to 4 deliveries
completed during what they believed was a peak pay window.

## Investigation
Support confirmed the peak pay window ended at 2:00 PM. The Dasher accepted
2 orders before 2:00 PM (eligible) and 2 orders after 2:00 PM (not eligible).

## Resolution
Two peak pay adjustments of $3 each ($6 total) were applied for the eligible
orders. The remaining 2 orders were confirmed ineligible.

## Lesson for KB
Peak pay applies to orders **accepted** during the window, not completed during
the window. The acceptance timestamp is the determining factor.

# Resolved Case: App Crash Causing Missed Deliveries

**Case ID:** RES-2024-0203
**Category:** Technical / App Issues
**Resolution:** Compensation credit issued

## Dasher's Issue
Dasher experienced repeated app crashes during a high-demand period, causing
them to miss 6 assigned orders. Their completion rate dropped from 94% to 88%.

## Investigation
Engineering confirmed a known bug in app version 5.112 affecting Android 14
devices. The crashes were logged server-side and correlated with the Dasher's
session.

## Resolution
- Completion rate was manually restored to 94%
- A $15 inconvenience credit was applied to the Dasher's account
- Dasher was advised to update to app version 5.113 which contained the fix

## Lesson for KB
App crashes affecting completion rate can be reviewed and reversed if the crash
is verified server-side. Dashers should always report technical issues immediately
via in-app Help and note the app version number.

# Resolved Case: Customer False Non-Delivery Claim

**Case ID:** RES-2024-0298
**Category:** Delivery Dispute
**Resolution:** Dasher protected — no action taken

## Dasher's Issue
Customer claimed order was never delivered. Dasher insisted it was delivered to
the correct address with a photo taken.

## Investigation
Support reviewed:
1. Delivery photo — confirmed order placed at correct door
2. GPS data — confirmed Dasher was at the delivery address at the recorded time
3. Customer history — customer had 3 prior non-delivery claims in 60 days

## Resolution
Claim was ruled as fraudulent. No action taken against the Dasher. Customer's
account was flagged for abuse review.

## Lesson for KB
Dashers are protected from unverified customer claims. GPS + delivery photos
are the primary evidence reviewed. Multiple claims by the same customer are
treated with higher scrutiny.
